NERDZ ’26 is the flagship techno-cultural fest of the School of Engineering Sciences & Technology, Jamia Hamdard University. It celebrates the idea that intellect, creativity, innovation, and fun go hand in hand. The fest combines technology, culture, music, theatre, dance, gaming, and innovation.

NERDZ is a two-day annual techno-cultural festival featuring technical competitions, cultural performances, and creative showcases.

Students from Jamia Hamdard University and other institutions can participate, subject to individual event guidelines.

The name NERDZ was chosen to redefine the stereotype of nerds and present them as curious, passionate, expressive, and fun.

NERDZ ’26 is held on 05 February 2026 and 06 February 2026.

Day 1 events take place on 05 February 2026.

The Inaugural Ceremony is held at the Convention Centre on Day 1.

The Hackathon is a high-intensity coding marathon held on Day 1 at Lab 401.

The 5G Ideathon is conducted on Day 1 at Lab 427 and focuses on futuristic ideas using 5G technology.

The Treasure Hunt takes place on Day 1 in the Stage Area and involves clues, logic, and teamwork.

The Solo and Duet Singing Competition is held on Day 1 on the Stage.

Dance Performances showcasing cultural and contemporary styles are held on Day 1 on the Stage.

Bait Bazi is an Urdu poetry competition conducted on Day 1 at the Archives.

Nukkad Natak is a street-theatre event held on Day 1 near the Stage and focuses on social issues.

Star Night is the main cultural attraction of Day 1 and is held on the Stage with music and live performances.

Day 2 events take place on 06 February 2026.

The UI Design Competition is held on Day 2 at the Convention Centre.

Bug Fest is a debugging competition conducted on Day 2 at the 1st Floor Lab.

The Tech Quiz is held on Day 2 at the Convention Centre.

Battle Zone is a gaming competition conducted on Day 2 on the Ground.

A Special Theatre Performance by SEST students is held on Day 2 at the Archives.

Battle of Bands takes place on Day 2 on the Stage and features competing music bands.

The Valedictory Ceremony is held on Day 2 at the Convention Centre and marks the conclusion of the fest.

Qawwali Night is held on Day 2 on the Stage and features soulful spiritual music performances.

NERDZ ’26 is organized with the support of faculty coordinators, student leads, performers, and guests, making it a celebration of intellect, creativity, and joy.
